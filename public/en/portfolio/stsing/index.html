Stefan Laser

I am a sociologist interested in interdisciplinary and international collaboration. I contribute to Science and Technology Studies and publish on waste, energy, trains and meat. I am part of the newly established collaborative research centre 'Virtual Living Realities'

My main theme right now is data centres: their local arrangement, building procedures, and global production networks enabling its operation.

Currently based in Bochum. Working from Hanoi, Vietnam.
