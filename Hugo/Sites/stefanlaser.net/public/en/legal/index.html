Stefan Laser

I am a sociologist with a soft spot for interdisciplinary problems. I contribute to Science and Technology Studies and publish on waste, energy, trains and meat. I am part of the newly established collaborative research centre 'Virtual Living Realities'

My main theme right now is data centres: their local arrangement, building procedures, and global production networks enabling its operation.

Currently based in Bochum, living in Erfurt. Soon moving to and working from Hanoi, Vietnam.
